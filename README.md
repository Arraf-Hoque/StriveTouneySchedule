# StriveTouneySchedule
JStriveTourneySchedule is a discord bot used for an easy way to see upcoming online tournaments for Guilty Gear -Strive-. I found it fairly tedious when looking as a tournament organizer and as a player to look for tournaments, when they were occuring and other crucial information about them. I took insporation from the Baiken discord bot and since a lot of players in the community discords used it, I decieded this would be a useful bot to make. 

# General Useage
$schedule - will bring up a schedule for both North American and EMEA Region Tournaments on Start.gg with their information and links
$help - a list of commands possible for use
$pinghawkeye - pings me in the discord as a joke
