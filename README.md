# StriveTouneySchedule
StriveTourneySchedule is a discord bot used for an easy way to see upcoming online tournaments for Guilty Gear -Strive-. It is fairly tedious when looking as a tournament organizer or as a player to look for tournaments on start.gg, when they were occuring and other crucial information about them. Inspiration taken from the Baiken discord bot a lot of players used in the community discords. 

# General Useage
$schedule - will bring up a schedule for both North American and EMEA Region Tournaments on Start.gg with their information and links

$help - a list of commands possible for use

$pinghawkeye - pings me in the discord as a joke
